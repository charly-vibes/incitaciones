## Pass 2: Scope and Atomicity

Focus on:
- One logical unit of work per issue
- Issues small enough for one session
- Issues not so small they should be bundled
- Clear boundaries and no overlap

Watch for:
- Oversized "build the whole system" issues
- Trivial issues that should be merged
- Overlapping responsibilities
- Refactor + feature bundles with unclear boundaries

Prefix findings with `SCOPE-`.
